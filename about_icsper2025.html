About ICSPER-2025

Welcome to ICSPER-2025

The 2ns international conference on Sustainable Power and Energy Research aims to be a strategic international forum for the exchange and dissemination of emerging technologies, research ideas, real time experiences and understanding of technology utilization in the field of electrical power and energy systems. The goal of the conference is to provide a stage for scientists, researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to address problems as well as solutions in the 21st Century for developing sustainable, stable, reliable, and resilient eco-friendly electrical system.

The international conference has been covering the topics of current interest in the area of energy management, data science and soft computing applications in power system, cyber security, renewable energy resources, microgrid, power electronics and drives, HV engineering, electric vehicle, energy storage system, electricity market, power system stability, control, operation and protection. The panel discussion and keynote sessions will provide an open platform for exchanging ideas on the recent development, applications and new standards in power electronics.

The ICSPER 2025 will be held in the campus of NIT Srinagar (Hybrid Mode), Jammu and Kashmir during July 11-12, 2025. It will be organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering, NIT Srinagar and NIT Warangal.
